About ToDoApp

ToDoApp is a simple and efficient task manager built using Flask and Bootstrap. It helps you stay organized by allowing you to:

  • Add and track your daily tasks
  • Edit and update your task details
  • Delete tasks when completed or no longer needed

This app was created as a personal productivity tool and to demonstrate the power of Flask and Bootstrap integration. It's lightweight, easy to use, and runs right in your browser.
